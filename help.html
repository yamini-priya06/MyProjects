Help & Support

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reserve a seat?

Log in as a student, navigate to the "Classes" page, select your classroom, and click on an available seat to reserve it.


Can I cancel or change my reserved seat?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your reservation up to 10 minutes before the class begins.


What if I forget my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.


Who do I contact for technical issues?

You can email our support team at seatease@gmail.com.
